01

Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide you with a great experience — nothing more. This includes data you provide directly and data generated through your use of the app.

👤

Account Info

Name, email address, profile photo, and authentication credentials.

📍

Location Data

Approximate location only when you explicitly grant permission.

📱

Device Info

Device type, OS version, and app version for debugging purposes.

📊

Usage Data

In-app interactions and feature usage to improve your experience.

02

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to operate, maintain, and improve the services we provide. We do not use your personal data for purposes beyond what is described here.

  • To create and manage your account securely
  • To personalize your in-app experience
  • To send important service updates and notifications
  • To analyze aggregate usage patterns and improve app performance
  • To respond to your support requests and feedback
  • To comply with legal obligations when required
03

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

🔐

Encryption

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest (AES-256).

🛡️

Secure Servers

Hosted on industry-leading cloud infrastructure with strict access controls.

🔑

Auth Security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. We support multi-factor authentication.

🔍

Regular Audits

We periodically review our security practices and update them as needed.

04

No Data Misuse

We are firmly committed to never misusing your personal data. The following practices are strictly prohibited within our organization:

  • Selling your personal data to advertisers or third parties
  • Using your data to build advertising profiles or target you with ads
  • Sharing your information with data brokers
  • Accessing your private content without explicit consent
  • Using your data for purposes not stated in this policy
  • Retaining your data beyond the period necessary to provide services

💬 Your trust is our most valuable asset. We will never monetize your personal data. Period.

05

Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of trusted third-party services to operate our platform. Each is carefully vetted and bound by data processing agreements.

  • MongoDB Atlas — secure cloud database hosting
  • Render — backend application hosting
  • Firebase — authentication and push notifications
  • Google Analytics — anonymized usage analytics only

We do not permit these providers to use your data for their own purposes beyond delivering services to us.

06

Your Rights

You have full control over your personal data. At any time, you may exercise the following rights by contacting us:

📋

Right to Access

Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.

✏️

Right to Correct

Update or correct inaccurate information in your account.

🗑️

Right to Delete

Request permanent deletion of your account and all associated data.

📦

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a machine-readable format (JSON/CSV).

07

Cookies & Tracking

Our mobile app does not use browser cookies. On our web platform, we use minimal, essential cookies only:

  • Session cookies to keep you logged in securely
  • Preference cookies to remember your app settings
  • No cross-site tracking cookies of any kind
  • No fingerprinting or supercookies
08

Policy Changes

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Notify you via in-app notification at least 7 days before changes take effect
  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Maintain an archive of previous policy versions on request

Continued use of the app after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
